# AGENTS.md - DPG Voter Engagement Platform

Democratic Party of Guam voter engagement and party operations application.

## Guardrails

- Treat this as a DPG-specific app, not a reskinned campaign clone.
- Do not add another campaign's private data, branding, scripts, prompts, or operating workflows.
- Keep DPG deployment/data/auth/secrets isolated.
- Build DPG flows around DPG's party operations needs.

## Frontend

- No emojis in UI.
- TypeScript strict/no `any`.
- Mobile-first.
- Tailwind v4.
